## Service Overview

Website redesign and migration services help businesses transform their outdated digital presence into modern, high-performing websites that drive results. Whether you're migrating from legacy platforms or completely reimagining your brand's online experience, I provide comprehensive solutions that prioritize performance, accessibility, and user experience.

## What's Included

### Discovery & Strategy

- Current website audit and performance analysis
- User research and competitor analysis
- Technical requirements assessment
- Migration planning and timeline development

### Design & Development

- Modern, responsive web design
- Performance optimization
- SEO-friendly architecture
- Cross-browser compatibility testing

### Migration Services

- Content migration and optimization
- URL structure planning and 301 redirects
- Database migration (if applicable)
- Third-party integrations setup

### Post-Launch Support

- Performance monitoring
- Bug fixes and adjustments
- Training and documentation
- Ongoing maintenance options

## Technologies & Platforms

I work with various platforms and technologies including:

- **Modern Frameworks**: React, Next.js, Gatsby
- **CMS Platforms**: Webflow, WordPress, Contentful, Sanity
- **E-commerce**: Shopify, WooCommerce, custom solutions
- **Static Site Generators**: 11ty, Hugo, Jekyll

## Process

1. **Discovery Phase** - Understanding your current setup and future goals
2. **Planning & Strategy** - Creating a comprehensive migration plan
3. **Design & Development** - Building your new website
4. **Testing & QA** - Ensuring everything works perfectly
5. **Migration & Launch** - Seamless transition to your new site
6. **Post-Launch Support** - Monitoring and optimization

## Typical Timeline

Most website redesign and migration projects take 6-12 weeks, depending on complexity and scope. I'll provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.
